Pretend News Organization

Pretend News Organization is a fake news site I made. I modeled its design off of a number of popular news sites, such as Fox News and CNN.

The purpose was to make a site that embodied current design trends, and efficiently laid out its content for a user to sift through.

It won the "Best Designed" award in an official competition among my peers.

DevMountain Survey System

DevMountain Survey System is a redesign of the original survey system that the coding bootcamp DevMountain used. At the time, the original survey system was unintuitive and very user unfriendly.

The goal of the project was to design and implement a more intuitive interface, as well as lay the groundwork to be able to track information from the surveys more easily.

Non-DevMountain students cannot access the site, however the link above has a walkthrough of the sites features.
